=== Cauliflower Burrito Bowls with Jalapeño Ranch ===
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy
Favorite: No
Link: https://dishingouthealth.com/cauliflower-burrito-bowls-with-jalapeno-ranch/
Image: data/urls/img/Cauliflower_Burrito_Bowls_with_Jalapeo_Ranch_a50c130a.jpg

Ingredients
[0.25] Cauliflower, medium head cut into florets
[0.75] tbspOlive Oil, extra-virgin divided
[0.38] tbspTaco Seasoning, homemade or store-bought
[0.25 cup] Red onion, diced
[0.5 tsp] tsp. adobo sauce (from a, can of chipotle peppers) (or 1 . chili powder)
[0.25] tspGround Cumin
[7 g] Edamame Beans Frozen ❄️, (15.5-.) can black drained but not rinsed
[0.5] Tomato, plum diced
[0.25 cup] Tinned Corn Kernels, frozen fire-roasted partially thawed
[0.5] tbspFresh coriander, chopped fresh
[0.25] tbspLime, fresh juice

Jalapeño Ranch
[0.25 cup] Greek Yogurt 2%, plain whole-milk Greek
[0.12] Jalapeno, and ribs removed
[0.06 cup] Olive Oil, extra-virgin
[0.06 cup] Fresh Coriander, packed fresh leaves
[0.5] tbspFresh Dill
[0.25] tspGarlic Powder, granulated
[0.12] tspOnion Powder
[0.12 tsp] Black Pepper, kosher salt + 1/4 .

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. In a large bowl, toss cauliflower with 2 Tbsp. of the oil and taco seasoning. (Note: most taco seasonings are salted, however if yours is not, add 1/2 tsp. kosher salt.)Arrange on a rimmed baking sheet, avoiding any overlap; bake for 30 to 35 minutes, tossing once halfway through, until tender and charred in some spots.
2. Meanwhile, heat remaining 1 Tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, cook until soft, about 3 to 5 minutes. Add adobo sauce, garlic, and cumin; cook 1 minute, stirring often. Add black beans, tomatoes, corn, and 1/2 tsp. kosher salt; simmer until mixture reduces, about 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in cilantro and lime juice.
3. Prepare Jalapeño Ranch by combining all dressing ingredients in a food processor; process until smooth.
4. Prepare bowls by dividing black bean mixture evenly into each of 4 bowls. Top evenly with cauliflower and additional toppings of choice. Drizzle Jalapeño Ranch dressing overtop.Note: all of these components are also amazing as taco fillings! Just tuck inside a toasted tortilla and voila.
